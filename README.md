## Nutrition Calculation at [Calorique](https://calorique.io/)

### Core Formulas
- **BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor)**: 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) − 5 × age + (5 men / -161 women)
- **TDEE**: BMR × activity multiplier (1.2 sedentary → 1.9 athlete)
- **Calorie burn**: kcal/min = (MET × 3.5 × weight kg) / 200

### MET Values
Per Compendium of Physical Activities (Ainsworth et al.):
- Walking moderate: 3.5 MET
- Running 8 mph: 13.5 MET
- Cycling moderate: 7.5 MET
- Swimming laps: 9.0 MET

### Macros
Standard ratios — adjust per goal:
- Maintain: 30/40/30 (P/C/F)
- Cut: 35/35/30
- Bulk: 25/50/25
